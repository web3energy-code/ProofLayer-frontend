✅ The ProofLayer Fix

01
One portable, verifiable identity
Your ProofLayer profile is your single source of truth across all of Web3. Every contribution you log is attached to your Phantom wallet address — immutable, portable, and owned entirely by you. Share it as your Web3 resume with any project, DAO, or employer.
02
Permanent, tamper-proof contribution records
Submit your contribution with a proof link. Sign it with your Phantom wallet. Our backend cryptographically verifies your identity and permanently stores the record. Even if the original platform disappears, your contribution record stays — attached to your wallet forever.

FAQ

Questions We Get Asked

Everything you need to know before connecting your wallet and starting your reputation journey.

Is ProofLayer free to use? +
Yes, completely free. There are no subscription fees, no gas fees, and no hidden costs to create a profile, submit contributions, or appear on the leaderboard. ProofLayer is built to be accessible to every contributor in Web3 regardless of how much SOL they hold.
Do I need SOL in my wallet to use ProofLayer? +
No. You only need a Phantom wallet — it does not need to have any SOL balance. The wallet is used purely for identity verification via message signing, not for any on-chain transactions. In future versions we plan to offer optional on-chain NFT badges which will require a small SOL amount for minting.
How does the signature verification work? +
When you click "Connect Wallet," Phantom generates a one-time nonce (random number) on our backend. Phantom then uses your wallet's private key to cryptographically sign a message containing that nonce. Our backend verifies the signature using your wallet's public key via the ed25519 algorithm. This proves you own the wallet without revealing your private key to anyone — including us. It's the same standard used across Web3 for wallet authentication.
Can I fake contributions? +
You cannot fake the wallet ownership — the cryptographic signature guarantees that. You can currently submit any proof URL, which we validate is accessible. Our roadmap includes community verification (where other contributors can challenge submissions), on-chain attestation for code contributions, and automated URL validation for common platforms like GitHub, X, and Realms. The system gets more robust over time.
What happens to my data if ProofLayer shuts down? +
Currently your contribution data is stored in MongoDB on our backend. Our roadmap includes storing contribution hashes on the Solana blockchain itself, which would make the records permanent and independent of our servers. We will give users full data export functionality before any shutdown scenario. Decentralizing the storage layer is a core part of our Q3 roadmap.
Can projects or DAOs look up my profile? +
Yes. Your ProofLayer profile is publicly viewable by anyone with your wallet address. The leaderboard is also fully public — no login required. Projects and DAOs can browse the leaderboard, filter by contribution type, and view any contributor's full profile. This is by design: your reputation should be discoverable by the people who could benefit from working with you.
How do I use ProofLayer on mobile? +
Install the Phantom app on iOS or Android. Open the Phantom app, tap the browser icon at the bottom, and navigate to the ProofLayer URL. The wallet connect button will trigger Phantom's native mobile signing flow seamlessly. Standard mobile browsers (Safari, Chrome) cannot connect to Phantom — you must use the built-in browser inside the Phantom app itself.
When will NFT badges be available? +
NFT badge minting via Metaplex is on our roadmap for a future release. When it launches, you will be able to mint your earned badges as Solana NFTs — making them visible in any Solana wallet and tradeable on secondary markets. Your existing badge history will be honoured and all earned badges will be mintable at no extra cost beyond standard Solana gas fees.
